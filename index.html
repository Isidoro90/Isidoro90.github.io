About
Isidoro is a Master of Public Policy from University of Chicago Harris School of Public Policy.
He holds a B.A in Economics from ITAM, where he graduated with the highest honors.
Prior enrolling at Harris, Isidoro worked for three years in Banco de Mexico (Mexico's Central Bank) Economic Research Department at the Regional Economic Analysis Division.
More recently, Isidoro worked during the 2017 summer at the World Bank Development Economics Impact Evaluation Unit (DIME).
He is passionate about economics, econometrics and impact evaluation.
